⚠️ IMPORTANT NOTES
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

• SECRET_KEY must be UNIQUE for your deployment - generate it!
• DEBUG must be set to False in production (SECURITY)
• DATABASE_URL must use PostgreSQL (not SQLite)
• ALLOWED_HOSTS must match your Vercel domain
• First deployment may be slower (installing dependencies)
• Subsequent deployments will be faster (incremental builds)
• Check Vercel logs if deployment fails
• CSS may not load on first visit (hard refresh fixes this)
